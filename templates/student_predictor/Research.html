Research

As a tool, the Student Predictor, enables students and educators to identify the current trajectory of their academic performance.

The following section explores a further set of possible questions that the Student Predictor can be used to answer.

  1. What are the implications of implementing such a prediction system and could it's uses be extended to use in other organizations such as schools and again what would the implications be here? Some ethics can be discussed too. (Ahmad Soni)
  2. How can we increase the reach of the product to a wider spread of subjects? (Nich Muir)
  3. What corrective measure(s) should be suggested based on the expected performance of the student? (Anthony Kubeka)
