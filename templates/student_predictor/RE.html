Research Explained

The overall outcome for this project is to enables students and educators to identify the current trajectory of their academic performance.
The Methods used in the research displayed under the research tab will be explained in this section.

Continuous meetings with our product owner directed us to three overall questions. Each question covers a main contributing aspect to a students success

  • Which subjects do students struggle most with?
  • Which features contribute the least to a student's success?
  • Which is more useful to student success, first or second year?
