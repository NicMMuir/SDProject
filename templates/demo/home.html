About

The Student Success Predictor exhibits one of the amazing ways machine learning is used behind the scenes to automatically learn and improve from experience without being explicity programmed. This website is built using the Django framework for Python.
The Student Predictor website predicts COMS 3 student outcomes using machine learning. By entering a students first year and second year average results, the outcome of a students third year result can be predicted.

The outcome is displayed in one of three categories. A 'Low Risk' outcome represents a student who is most likely to do well in their third year. With a possible final average greater than 60%. A 'Medium Risk' outcome represents a student who is likely to pass. However, such a student is a borderline case and has a prospect of failing as well. With a possible final average between 45% and 60%. And finally a 'High Risk' outcome represents a student who is likely to fail. With a possible final average less than 45%. A greater attentiveness would be required by facilitators and student alike.

What does it do?

Use the Student Success Predictor to input the current marks of a university student and get a prediction of their future marks. The student predictor makes it easy to identify students who are at risk of failing.
On the Predict Student Success page,you are prompted to enter details required to predict the outcome of a students third year coms result, then the final result will be displayed on the Show All Students Data page.
If you wish to predict a large number of students outcomes all at once, it can be done on the Predict Multiple Student Success page.
The Show All Students Data page displays the final prediction of the students outcome as a Low Risk, Medium Risk or High Risk.
To logout the user must simply press the logout button on the Logout page.
